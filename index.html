About us

The language school "French is easy" started its activity in 2008. Since then, we have been actively developing, continuously analyzing our work, quickly trying to make adjustments to the educational process to improve it. The dynamics of growth in all its aspects is extremely important for us: from educational and organizational to household. For teaching, we have developed our methodology with a combination of all aspects of speech: spoken, grammatical, listening and written. We place the greatest emphasis on spoken language.
